## How to Change the Video

1. **Choose a video** from the alternatives above or find your own on:
   - Pexels Videos: https://www.pexels.com/search/videos/manufacturing/
   - Pixabay Videos: https://pixabay.com/videos/search/industrial/
   - Videvo: https://www.videvo.net/free-video/industrial/

2. **Get the direct video URL** (must be a direct .mp4 link)

3. **Update `index.html`** line 68:
   ```html
   <source src="YOUR_VIDEO_URL_HERE" type="video/mp4">
   ```

4. **Test the video** by opening index.html in your browser

## Video Requirements

✅ **Format:** MP4 (H.264)
✅ **Resolution:** Minimum 1920x1080 (Full HD)
✅ **Theme:** Industrial, Manufacturing, Gears, Machinery
✅ **Duration:** 10-30 seconds (will loop)
✅ **License:** Free to use commercially
✅ **File Size:** Under 10MB recommended for fast loading

---

## Tips for Best Performance

1. **Video should loop seamlessly** - Choose videos without obvious start/end points
2. **Keep it under 10MB** - Compress if needed using tools like HandBrake
3. **Use a poster image** - Add `poster="image.jpg"` attribute for faster perceived loading
4. **Mobile considerations** - Some mobile browsers may not autoplay videos
5. **Fallback background** - The site has a light background as fallback
